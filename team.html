Our Team

We're a digital agency located in Bloomington, Indiana. We are a group of passionate designers, developers, and videographers who love creating engaging content across different forms of digital media. We help businesses develop solutions to connect with their customers online. Usually that takes the form of web design/development and video production.

Our small team is able to move fast from the process of sketching to protypes to design and implementation. By having a small adaptable team we are able to meet one on one with clients and create something beautiful together.
